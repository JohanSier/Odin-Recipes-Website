Bandeja Paisa

Colombian Cuisine

Description

Bandeja Paisa is a traditional Colombian dish from the Antioquia region. Known for its hearty and diverse components, it typically includes rice, beans, ground beef, chicharr贸n (fried pork belly), fried plantain, chorizo, arepa, avocado, and a fried egg. Bandeja Paisa is a celebration of Colombia's rich culinary culture, offering a variety of flavors and textures in one plate.

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Red beans
  • Ground beef
  • Chicharr贸n (fried pork belly)
  • Fried plantain
  • Chorizo
  • Avocado
  • Fried egg
  • Hogao (Colombian tomato and onion sauce)
  • Lime (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the red beans in a pot with water until tender. Season with salt and hogao.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the ground beef until browned and fully cooked.
  3. Fry the chicharr贸n until crispy.
  4. Fry the plantain slices until golden and caramelized.
  5. Cook the chorizo in a pan until fully cooked.
  6. Prepare the arepas by grilling or frying them until golden brown.
  7. Cook white rice as per usual method.
  8. Fry an egg sunny-side up.
  9. Assemble the dish: On a large plate, serve a portion of rice, beans, ground beef, chicharr贸n, fried plantain, chorizo, and arepa. Add sliced avocado and top with the fried egg.
  10. Serve hot, optionally garnished with a lime wedge.
